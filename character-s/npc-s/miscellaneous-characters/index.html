Relationships

  • Eran Underfoot, brother of Hayn Underfoot - Killed by Kent prior to the start of the campaign.

Enemies

  • Idrisir, a grand wizard who resided in a tower in the city of Brass. Kept a Unicorn captive, which the party freed. The party broke into his tower, ultimately killing him on their way back out of the Elemental Plane of Fire.

  • Kan Regz, a hobgoblin serving Bane, God of Tyranny. Killed by the party in the underdark under Covin. Given reward by Lord Redowith.

Shopkeeper's

  • Antony Castillo, hispanic-looking "shopkeeper" who pretended to have useful goods outside Drovic. Caused a fight with some off-duty guards, including Gert, whom he sold a fake boner potion.

  • Andor Castillo, hispanic-looking Antony's brother. Bard of King Drovic IV. Met during the King's Ball, and subsequently rescued after he had been taken.

Guards

  • Gert, guard who attacked the party outside of Drovic.

Political

  • Lord Redowith, a lord of Covin. Rewarded the party with invitations to the King's Ball in Drovic after they defeated Kan Regz, a local hobgoblin leader..

Other

  • Remirez Castillo, hispanic-looking, father of both Antony and Andor; part of the crew that created the Manawar Armada, the large airship that patrols around Drovic. Worked alongside it's inventor, Hominom^Remirez
  • Hominom Dryadalis, inventor of the Manawar Armada, worked alongside Remirez. Banished from Drovic after refusing to cease experiments. Kept a journal, titled: "Divinity - The Original Sin".
  • A unicorn, freed by the party on their way out of the city of Brass. Held captive by a wizard, Idrisir. Afterwards, this unicorn decided to reside in the Great Forests surrounding Boab, acting as it's protector.
